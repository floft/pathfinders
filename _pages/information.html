Information

The Cascade Eagles Pathfinder Club has regular weekly meetings during the school year. For these meetings, we meet at the Skagit Adventist Academy gym on Tuesday evenings from 6:30 - 8:00 pm. See the calendar for exact dates we will be meeting. We also have several other activities throughout the school year such as campouts, share-your-faith programs, and Conference events.

Questions?

Please Contact Us if you have any further questions about joining the Cascade Eagles Pathfinder Club.
