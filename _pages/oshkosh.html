Oshkosh 2009

The Cascade Eagles had a wonderful time attending the 2009 Pathfinder International Camporee held August 11-15, 2009, in Oshkosh, Wisconsin. This 5-day event was sponsored by the North American Division of Seventh-day Adventist.

The theme was "Courage to Stand" and the evening programs featured drama and music based on the Bible Book of Esther.

Attending this event provided youth the experience of meeting other Pathfinders from all over the world and growing spiritually by participating in a variety of classes, worships, and activities designed to encourage youth to grow closer to God.

We are now looking ahead to the 2014 Oshkosh International Camporee.
